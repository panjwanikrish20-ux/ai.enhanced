About Me

Hey! I'm Krish Panjwani, a self-taught web designer and front-end developer from India. I discovered my passion for the web when I realized that a screen could be a canvas — and I've been painting on it ever since.

As a fresher stepping into the industry, I bring something that experience alone can't teach: raw curiosity, relentless energy, and a hunger to prove myself through every line of code and every design choice I make.

I specialize in crafting visually stunning, responsive, and performance-focused websites. From pixel-perfect layouts to smooth micro-interactions — I care about the details that most people never notice but always feel.

My journey has been entirely self-driven. No classroom handed me these skills — I built them through late nights, tutorials, failures, and breakthroughs. That hustle is baked into everything I create.

I'm currently seeking my first professional opportunity where I can contribute real value, grow fast, and build things worth showing off. If you're looking for someone who shows up with passion and ships with precision — let's talk.
